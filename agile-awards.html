Agile Awards

Have you been successful to bring agility to your business? What magic was applied, How did you achieve it? Industry and community would love to hear your transformation journey and learn from your journey on how to adopt a right balance between Agile Practices, Frameworks, and Tools based on Agile Manifesto’s values and Principles.

If your answer to the above-mentioned question is “YES” then nominate your organization today for the Agile Award 2021 and let the world learn from your organization success.


Continuing with our legacy, this year we are back again with the 2nd edition of Agile Awards as part of annual flagship event AgileNCR 2021 Live virtual. It brings you the right platform to showcase your organization’s success in front of well renowned industry professionals.

At Xebia, it is our constant endeavor to recognize the inventive & visionary organizations who have taken the leap and a step forward in improving their methods of delivery, creating more value for the customer, generating a higher degree of ROI, embracing change by using alternative methods and approach in their thinking and execution with the Agile Manifesto as a cornerstone, and have exemplified passion as well as audacity in accepting the challenge to adopt the discipline.


These awards will help us bring out the unique learning, identified approaches and true transformation stories of business Agility rather than just transition to Agile ways of working.


This is your opportunity to win recognition on this premium global community platform for the great work that you and your teams are delivering for your organization.

Judging Rules

Judging will be undertaken by an independent panel of judges appointed by Agile NCR 2021 organizing committee who will be bound by a confidentiality agreement that bans them from externally discussing or sharing the information from the entries.

Sponsoring organizations will not be allowed to get involved with the judging process.

Eligibility Criteria

The Agile Awards 2021 are open to all organizations, which have experienced (or experiencing) Agile Transformation in the duration of last 18 months (Jan-2020 to Jun-2021), to deliver organizational excellence and outstanding business outcomes.


There is no minimum requirement or restriction on the scale of the project or on the type of Framework being used. Each entry will be judged on the merits of the program implementation and results achieved against the organization’s strategic objectives.


Please do contact us at email if you require any further assistance as you prepare your entry. Make these Awards a priority now to ensure that you do not miss out on this wonderful opportunity.


We are eagerly looking forward to your participation in the Agile Awards 2021.
